Legendary Card: Sparky

I hate giving her and seeing her at the last spot again and again. For some reason, Supercell hasn’t buffed her for some reason which is pretty unfair.

Sparky is basically a High-risk High-reward card. What this means is that she can be countered very easily and can give opponent good advantage making her a risky choice but her high damage of 1300 at Tournament Standards (Highest Damage per hit) makes her each hit highly rewarding.

As people have recently started to realize, Sparky is better as a threat card and defensive card (when couple with Tornado) than a Win-Condition. She is best used with Giant, especially in Spell-Bait archetype.

A balance change I suggest is to decrease her cost to 5 elixir, decrease hit speed to 4 seconds and increase damage to 1400. These changes make rocketing her a negative trade, gives her faster recharge, more damage resulting in higher DPS and altogether making her better.

The release of Mega Knight just added another fair counter to Sparky. He is just too tanky for her to kill him. Lets pray that she receives a buff soon.
