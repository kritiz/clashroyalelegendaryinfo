Legendary Card: Night Witch

This card has taken a big drop on the list. But at last she is finally balanced, not OP anymore.

After nerf, she now fits only in one archetype i.e. Beatdown extremely well while doing average in other archetypes which totally unlike before where she was a Jack of all Trades.

In beatdown, she is strong because the bats can snow ball behind a tank and can deal tons of damage.

Killing her will still spawn 2 more bats along with the bats already present making her deadly.

Night Witch is actually a great counter to MK on defense, killing him alone due to her Bat Spawn without any hit and +3 elixir trade. This has made her a good choice again.
